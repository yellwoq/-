In a distant sea area, there is a crescent shaped continent, which has distinct terrain, barren land in the north, and no grass. All day long, it is covered with black fog and miasma. There are a group of monsters. Among them, they kill each other, plunder resources, follow the survival law of the strong, respect the strength, the strong possess everything, and the weak will linger and survive, Depending on the strong, and above them, there is a strong man named "demon night emperor". It is the king of the monster race, with the unique strength and strong men around him. He has always wanted to invade the south, rob resources and unify the new moon continent...
